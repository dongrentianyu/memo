# 在创造性工作中执着于产出往往适得其反

原文：[Fixation on outcome in creative work is often self-defeating (andymatuschak.org)](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/zWhZZuWVtFXsPNL8Ph3po7R8BEDztTSsN1X)
